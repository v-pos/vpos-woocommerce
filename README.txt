vPOS - Payment Gateway is a payment solution for Angolan based businesses.

== Description ==

It is an easy and secure payment solution for WordPress/WooCommerce for Angolan businesses, adapted to the COVID-19 period.
vPOS will transform the way you receive payments online, thus allowing you to
your business takes advantage of the thousands of internet users who wish to shop in a way
easy, fast and secure over the Internet.

= Contribute =

vPOS Payment Gateway is developed and supported by Next Business Solution, the creators of vpos.ao.
